Shiatsu Massage

Shiatsu massage emphasizes pressure massage on specific parts such as meridians and acupuncture points, or kneading specific parts to relax deep muscles, so it is not suitable for applying essential oils or lotions. If the skin surface is too slippery, the pressure on the acupoints may be inaccurate, so you can massage directly with your clothes on.

Oil Pressure Massage

Oil pressure massage usually adds essential oils or lotions, and the hands are in direct contact with the skin over a large area. This is a massage designed for relaxation. Adding essential oils will make the massage smoother and more comfortable, reducing discomfort. If you use special natural plant essential oils, you can also bring a deeper soothing effect and relax your body and mind. Oil pressure massage requires the person being massaged to take off their clothes and apply an appropriate amount of essential oil to help moisturize the skin and prevent colds. Essential oils have a releasing and stress-relieving effect.

Because of results like these, and its portability, chair massage is a popular option for employers who want to improve productivity or morale.
